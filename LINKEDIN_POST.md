## Posting Strategy

### Best Time to Post
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM (your timezone)
- **Avoid**: Mondays (inbox overload), Fridays (weekend mode), weekends

### Engagement Tips
1. **Pin a comment** with additional resources:
   - GitHub repository link
   - Documentation highlights
   - Quick start video (if available)

2. **Respond to all comments** within first 2 hours

3. **Share in relevant groups**:
   - AI/ML communities
   - Developer tools groups
   - Claude/Anthropic user communities

4. **Use carousel format** (LinkedIn allows up to 10 slides):
   - Slide 1: Headline announcement
   - Slide 2: Problem statement
   - Slide 3: Solution overview
   - Slide 4-6: Key features (one per slide)
   - Slide 7-8: Real results/testimonials
   - Slide 9: Call to action
   - Slide 10: Thank you + resources

### Hashtag Strategy
- **3-5 hashtags** maximum for LinkedIn
- Mix of **broad** (#AI, #OpenSource) and **niche** (#ClaudeCode, #DevTools)
- Place at the end of post, not throughout

### Follow-up Actions
- **Day 2**: Share user feedback/early adoption stories
- **Week 1**: Technical deep-dive article
- **Week 2**: Video demo or tutorial
- **Month 1**: Case study with metrics
